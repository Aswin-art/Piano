Game Instructions

  • Choose your song before play the games, if you are not picking any song the game wouldn't start
  • Press key D, F, J, K to hit the tiles and get a score
  • The score will be increased if player click the keys when the tiles is touched the border
  • Player fails if the score below 50% in the end
  • Player can increase or decrease the speed by clicking CTRL and (+) or CTRL and (-)
